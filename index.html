Therefore whoever hears these sayings of Mine, and does them, I will liken him to a wise man who built his house on the rock. (Matthew 5:1 thru 7:29)

The Helper, the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in My name, He will teach you all things, and bring to your remembrance all things I said to you. (John 13:31 thru 17:26)

Amen. (Matthew 28:18-20)

I send you out as sheep in the midst of wolves. (Matthew chapter 10)

By your words you will be justified, and by your words you will be condemned. (Matthew 12:33-37)

Now when the congregation had broken up, many of the Jews and devout proselytes followed Paul and Barnabas, who, speaking to them, persuaded them to continue in the grace of God. (Acts 13:42-52)
