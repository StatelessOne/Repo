This is the covenant I will make with the house of Israel…
  • I will put My laws in their mind and write them on their hearts; and I will be their God, and they shall be My people.
  • None of them shall teach his neighbor, and none his brother, saying ‘Know the Lord.’ for all shall know Me, from the least of them to the greatest of them.
  • I will be merciful to their unrighteousness and their sins and their lawless deeds I will remember no more. (Hebrews 8:10-12)

I did not come to call the righteous, but sinners, to repentance (Matthew 9:9-13)

Do not marvel that I said to you, ‘You must be born again.’ (John 3:3-21)

Take my yoke upon you and learn from Me. (Matthew 11:25-30)

Therefore whoever hears these sayings of Mine, and does them, I will liken him to a wise man who built his house on the rock. (Matthew 5:1 thru 7:29)

The Helper, the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in My name, He will teach you all things, and bring to your remembrance all things I said to you. (John 13:31 thru 17:26)
